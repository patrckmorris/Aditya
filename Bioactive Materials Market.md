<p data-start="1534" data-end="1583"><strong data-start="1534" data-end="1583">Market Definition: Bioactive Materials Market</strong></p>
<p data-start="1585" data-end="2244">Bioactive materials are substances that have a specific biological effect and can interact with biological systems to promote a desired response. These materials can be synthesized or natural and are typically used in biomedical applications such as tissue regeneration, wound healing, and bone reconstruction. They are most commonly used in the form of scaffolds, coatings, or implants to support cellular activity, encourage tissue regeneration, and facilitate healing processes. The bioactive materials market is witnessing rapid growth due to increasing demand for innovative, biocompatible solutions in the medical, dental, and pharmaceutical industries.</p>
