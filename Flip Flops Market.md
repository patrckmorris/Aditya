<p data-start="683" data-end="1234"><strong data-start="683" data-end="705">Market Definition:</strong> <a href="https://straitsresearch.com/report/flip-flops-market">Flip flops</a> are a type of casual footwear characterized by a flat sole and a Y-shaped strap that passes between the first and second toes, with the other end resting on the sides of the foot. They are typically made from materials such as rubber, plastic, leather, and foam, offering comfort and breathability. Flip flops are especially popular in warm weather, on beaches, and for casual or outdoor activities. They are also favored for their easy-to-wear design and affordability, making them a go-to option for many consumers.</p>
