<p data-start="1814" data-end="1835"><strong data-start="1814" data-end="1835">Market Definition</strong></p>
<p data-start="1837" data-end="2506">Silicone sealants are polymer-based products used to fill gaps, joints, or seams in a wide range of substrates, preventing the ingress of water, air, or other contaminants. They are primarily known for their excellent weatherproofing properties, including high resistance to extreme temperatures, moisture, and UV radiation. Silicone sealants are commonly used in construction, automotive, aerospace, and electronics industries for applications such as window sealing, door sealing, and bonding components. The versatility and durability of silicone sealants have made them a preferred choice in industries requiring long-lasting and high-performance sealing solutions.</p>
