It originated in China and is a common ingredient in East Asian and Southeast Asian cuisines. Known for its high protein content, tofu is considered a healthy food option and is widely consumed as a meat substitute in vegan and vegetarian diets. Tofu is available in various forms, including silken, firm, and extra firm, and is often used in a variety of dishes, including soups, stir-fries, and salads. The global popularity of plant-based diets and the increasing shift toward sustainable and healthy eating habits contribute to tofu's rising demand.</p>
