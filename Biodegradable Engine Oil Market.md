<p data-start="1648" data-end="1669"><strong data-start="1648" data-end="1669">Market Definition</strong></p>
<p data-start="1671" data-end="2286">Biodegradable engine oils are oils that decompose naturally in the environment over time, causing minimal environmental damage. They are made from renewable plant-based sources or synthetic materials that are designed to break down efficiently when exposed to environmental elements such as water, soil, and air. These oils are used in internal combustion engines in various applications including automobiles, industrial machinery, and marine engines. Biodegradable engine oils offer enhanced performance with minimal environmental impact, making them a preferred choice for eco-conscious consumers and industries.</p>
