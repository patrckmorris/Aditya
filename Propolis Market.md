<p data-start="1159" data-end="1197"><strong data-start="1159" data-end="1197">Market Definition: Propolis Market</strong></p>
<p data-start="1199" data-end="1676">Propolis is a natural resin collected by bees from tree buds and sap flows, mixed with beeswax and enzymes. It is used for a variety of purposes, most notably in traditional medicine, cosmetics, and as a food supplement. Propolis is known for its wide range of health benefits, such as its ability to boost the immune system, heal wounds, and fight infections. Due to its high antimicrobial properties, propolis is also used in skincare products and as a preservative in food.</p>
