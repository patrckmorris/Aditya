<h3 data-start="560" data-end="582">Market Definition:</h3>
<p data-start="583" data-end="1167"><a href="https://straitsresearch.com/report/coated-steel-market">Coated steel</a> refers to steel that has been coated with a layer of material such as paint, zinc, or plastic to provide resistance to corrosion, improve durability, and enhance aesthetic appeal. The coatings used in these steel products can include galvanizing, powder coating, and organic coatings, among others, making coated steel an essential material in construction, automotive, and household appliances, among other sectors. As a versatile product, coated steel plays a crucial role in increasing the longevity of metal components and enhancing performance in harsh environments.</p>
